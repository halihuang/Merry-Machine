About
MerryMachine is a news-filtering site that divides news articles from various sources into several different catagories: positive, negative, and political. Ofcourse, we don't have a human sift through all the articles; we have a machine do it! This allows for you to read the most up-to-date content from some of the top sources in the U.S. (and hopefully from other nations in upcoming updates).
