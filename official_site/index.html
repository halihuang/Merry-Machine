Learning Positivity

Are you tired of being depressed when watching the news?

Merry Machine aggregrates news from reputable sources all across the web and utilizes machine learning algorithms to identify positive news




Our Goal

Of course, we don't want to distract from the current civil rights and humanitarian events going on in the world today. However, it is important that we not only look at the negative news which the media illustrates, but to look at the strides that are being made in these areas and in other areas. Through this website, we hope to remind you that amazing people and events are still out there, that we are progressing towards to a better future. If a machine can identify happiness in our world, why can't we?

Disclaimer: We do not 'steal' news from any publication out there. We only provide the links, title and provided summary of the news that our program classifies.

About
MerryMachine is a news-filtering site that divides news articles from various sources into several different catagories: positive, negative, political, and neutral. Ofcourse, we don't have a human sift through all the articles; we have a machine do it! This allows for you to read the most up-to-date content from some of the top sources in the U.S. (and hopefully from other nations in upcoming updates).
